“Wonderful place to stay while visiting Cape Town”

CBV has 3 units and we were shown all 3. Each was lovely and well decorated. My wife and I stayed in the Penthouse, a multilevel unit with the bedroom on the top floor of the entire building. The views from both our bedroom and the living/dining/kitchen area one floor below were breathtaking. We also had an outdoor patio and reflection pool as part of the unit but were unwilling to take a dip because the air and water temperature were a little on the cold side. The bed was quite comfortable as were the dining and living areas.

Trevor, the owner, is a friendly and warm host, eager to make sure everything was to our satisfaction. He started off with a tour of the unit and showing how to open and close the exterior shutters on most of the doors and windows and how to operate everything. He will make your breakfast every morning but I was content to settle just for his squeezing oranges to make fresh juice while I cooked the eggs and toast. He also stocked our unit with various items I had asked be included. He provided us advice on staying safe in Cape Town, places and sights to see, and directions to wherever we wanted to go. A couple of nights he drove us to restaurants in Camps Bay and picked us up when done. I would stay at CBV again and whenever I might visit Cape Town in the future. It is a fabulous place to stay. The one caveat is that it is not for the handicapped (except possibly the studio unit) as the entire building is built on the flanks of Table Mountain and requires the navigation of multiple sets of stairs every day. However, my wife and I are still reasonably fit and occasionally hike so stairs were not a problem for us."

July, 2018
Richard , USA
