Penthouse

The 150 sq.m.penthouse is on the uppermost part of the property, with direct private access to Table Mountain Reserve from a mountain facing lawn. Open plan living, terrace and pool areas merge with awesome, all-round beach, ocean and mountain views for an on top of the world, majestically beautiful environment and ambience.

Facilites include a magnificent, ocean facing, upstairs maple floor bedroom, en suite, with terrace, open plan fully fitted kitchen, louvre shutters, solara awnings, leather suites and large travertine italian tiles. The penthouse has remote control airconditioning, tv and sound system, music systems, wireless internet, alarm, intercom, outdoor shower and daily servicing.
