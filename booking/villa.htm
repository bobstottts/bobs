Villa

The villa has exceptional views, spacious living areas and patios, merging with a private, peaceful garden and sparkling pool.

Facilities include two spacious, ocean facing upstairs maple floor bedrooms, en suite, each with large balconies, open plan fully fitted kitchen, louvre shutters, solara awnings, leather suites and large travertine italian tiles.

The villa has remote control airconditioning, music systems, tv, wireless internet, alarm, intercom, outdoor shower and is serviced daily.
