What is a Wallet Address?

A wallet address is like a bank account where you can keep your tokens and cryptocurrencies. It is also where anyone can do various transactions with you through a public key automatically generated per transaction, and you can keep it safe by keeping its private key out of reach of other people.

Do note that like your bank account, this is your only means to send and receive tokens and cryptocurrencies. Be reminded that you need to paste this wallet address on the purchase form to buy tokens.
