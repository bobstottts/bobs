My wife Toni and I rented the Free Spirit SS for a week and enjoyed a trip through Death Valley, to Mammoth Lakes then Yosemite and on to Lake Tahoe and Reno. We loved everything about the Free Spirit, easy to drive plenty of space for two people, easy to hook up to the facilities at the KOA and much more. We took two bicycles on a bike rack and two sets of golf clubs in the back storage. We were most impressed with the way the Free Spirit was outfitted. Everything we needed was provided. We could have moved into the Free Spirit with only our cloths since it was outfitted with virtually every thing you would need. No stone left unturned....so to speak. Everything first class.

Roy, Tulsa OK
